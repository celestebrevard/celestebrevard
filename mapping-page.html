Project Goal:

To visualize the atrocities taking place in Syria that are impacting the populace. The information is provided by the Syria Watch app and the Syrian Emergency Task Force.

This project is part of a larger initative to create a Dashboard as a resource to the public, policymakers, and the media to better comprehend the situation in Syria.
